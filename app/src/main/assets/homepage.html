Private Browsing

What Private Browsing Does

Your browsing history, cookies, and site data won't be saved after you close all private tabs.

Downloads and Bookmarks

Files you download and bookmarks you create will be kept even after closing private tabs.

Your Activity is Still Visible To

Websites you visit, your internet service provider, and your network administrator can still see your browsing activity.

Note: Private browsing doesn't make you anonymous online. For enhanced privacy, consider using a VPN.
